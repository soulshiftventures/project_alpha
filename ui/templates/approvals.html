Approval Guidelines

When reviewing approvals:

  • Low risk - Read-only operations, can typically be auto-approved
  • Medium risk - Write operations or cost-incurring actions, review details before approving
  • High risk - Bulk operations, external notifications, or significant cost, careful review required
  • Critical risk - Irreversible operations, must be manually approved with rationale

Approved items with connectors can optionally be promoted to live mode, allowing real API calls. This requires valid credentials and policy compliance.
